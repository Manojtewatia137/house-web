SATYAM PROPERTIES

SATYAM PROPERTIES Group is a multi diversified group majorly involved in Real Estate, Infrastructure, Hospitality, Sports and Entertainment. VVIP Infratech Ltd. popularly known as VVIP is a renowned name that excelled into prominence over a decade in the respective sectors. The group is built on dedication, dynamism and expertise. VVIP Group carries a legacy that goes over 3 decades. In real estate, the group launched its first residential project VVIP Addresses at Rajnagar Extension, Ghaziabad. As imagined, it came out to be a mark of pride not only in terms of its location, area, amenities, construction quality, green area but also the gentry or the quality of people residing in the project...! Since then the group has delivered numerous commercial and residential projects in the city of Ghaziabad and Noida. In our endeavour to set new trends and with the confidence/trust of our customers we are continuing our journey on the path to become an icon of pride for the generations to come.

Vision & Mission

SATYAM PROPERTIES Group aims to create top-notch quality homes equipped with all amenities at all levels of affordability. We envision our projects by seeking first the customers needs and preferences.

We stay focused to provide superior service to all the residents. We believe in building a harmonious relationship with our customers, employees, and vendors to achieve accelerating growth. All our projects are planned and executed using innovative technology, superior layouts, and aesthetically pleasing designs to provide perfect spaces for every person.
